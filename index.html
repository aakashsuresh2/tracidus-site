FAQ

Does this send data anywhere? ▾

No. The simulation runs entirely inside your browser. No data is ever transmitted or stored outside your device.

Can I export results? ▾

Yes. You can download the radar chart as a PNG, export a full JSON report of the analysis, or simply copy a summary to your clipboard.

How is link reputation determined? ▾

For demonstration purposes, TRACIDUS uses a local, rule-based engine to simulate link analysis. It checks for common link shortening services, suspicious keywords, and domain patterns to generate a risk score. It does not perform live lookups.
